How to make a gingerbread house ...

Assembly time: 40 minutes to 2 hours
Helpful rating: 10% helpful

Are you wondering how to make a gingerbread house that already has instructions for you? Well here is your step to step guide on how to not mess up your gingerbread house!

  • Things You'll Need

  • Money
  • Car
  • Gingerbread House Kit

  1. Get in your car and drive to Target.

    Once there, look for the gingerbread houses in the back of the store without stopping to look for other items.

  2. From there, pick out one of the many gingerbread houses that Target has like a barn, stadium, normal gingerbread house, etc.

    Then buy it at the register, and drive home.

  3. When home, get all of your supplies out of the box.

    Create a clean space to start your decorating and building of the gingerbread house.

    Above are the supplies from the gingerbread house barn kit

  4. Then, follow the instructions on the back of the box.

    Assemble your gingerbread house.

  5. Finally, take pictures of the gingerbread house.

    Watch it slowly fall apart, eat the rest of the candy left, and admire your gingerbread house or hate it because you dislike your designs!
